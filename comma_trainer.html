Comma Logic Trainer

3 Levels of Difficulty

Compound Sentences (Level 1)

The Golden Rule

Formula: [Main Clause] , [Conjunction] [Main Clause]

"I wanted shoes, but I had no money."

Complex Sentences (Level 1 & 2)

Scenario A: The Opener

If the Sub-clause comes first, use a comma.

"If you study, you will pass."

Scenario B: The Closer

If the Sub-clause comes second, NO comma.

"You will pass if you study."

Interrupters (Level 3)

The Scoop Rule

If you can "scoop" the phrase out and the sentence still makes sense, use commas on BOTH sides.

"My dad, a tall man, plays basketball."
